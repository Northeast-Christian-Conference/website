Important Conference Information

Registration: Online registration will open on July 29, 2023 at 9am ET. Registration is REQUIRED for all participants, including children. Deadline for submitting registration requests is Aug 31, 2023. Deadline for changes and cancellations is Sep 10, 2023.

Format: In-person meeting. Messages will be livestreamed. All attendees must attend full-time. Day trip visits cannot be accommodated this year.

Location: Harvey Cedars Bible Conference (12 Cedars Ave, Harvey Cedars, NJ 08008)

Lodging: There are both on-site and off-site lodging options this year.

For on-site lodging arrangement:

  • Families: Each family will stay together in 1 room. While the lodging room is not shared with other families, please assume that the bathroom may be a shared bathroom
  • Individuals (those not attending with a family): Individuals should assume that they will be assigned a lodging room and a bathroom that are shared with other individuals

All attendees are encouraged to have their meals on-site. The costs of all the meals are already included in the conference fees.

Children’s Ministry: Children’s Ministry, including nursery, will be available on-site.
