<p>
<h2><strong><u>Important Conference Information</u></strong></h2>
</p>
<p>
<strong>Registration: <u>Online registration will open on July 29, 2023 at 9am ET.</u></strong> Registration is <strong><u>REQUIRED</u></strong> for all participants, including children. Deadline for submitting registration requests is Aug 31, 2023. Deadline for changes and cancellations is Sep 10, 2023.
<br><br>
<strong>Format:</strong> In-person meeting. Messages will be livestreamed. All attendees must attend full-time. Day trip visits cannot be accommodated this year.
<br><br>
<strong>Location:</strong> Harvey Cedars Bible Conference (12 Cedars Ave, Harvey Cedars, NJ 08008)<br><br>
<strong>Lodging:</strong> There are both on-site and off-site lodging options this year.<br><br>
For on-site lodging arrangement: <br>
<ul>
<li><u>Families:</u> Each family will stay together in 1 room. While the lodging room is not shared with other families, please assume that the bathroom may be a shared bathroom</li>
<li><u>Individuals (those not attending with a family):</u> Individuals should assume that they will be
assigned a lodging room and a bathroom that are shared with other individuals </li>
</ul>
<br>
All attendees are encouraged to have their meals on-site. The costs of all the meals are already included in the conference fees.<br><br>
<strong>Children’s Ministry:</strong> Children’s Ministry, including nursery, will be available on-site.
<br><br>
</p>
