Welcome!

Thank you for choosing to pay your registration fee online.

The registration fee is $50 for EACH person (max $150 per family).
Your registration fee can be applied towards your meal and lodging costs, or can be an offering towards conference expenses such as use of facilities, gifts to the speakers, rental equipment and children's class supplies.

The deadline to submit the registration fee is 48 hours from the receipt of your confirmation email, or your registration will automatically be CANCELLED.

Online Payment Instructions

– Enter your Family ID and choose your registration option below.
– Click the 'Registration Fee' button to open the secure PayPal checkout.
– On the PayPal screen, you can sign in to your account or choose to pay via debit or credit card.
