Before you begin, please carefully read through the following important changes to the registration process this year.


  • All in-person attendees are expected to attend full-time. We will not be able to accommodate part-time attendees this year.
  • EVERY ADULT AND CHILD must register in order to attend the conference in-person. Day trip visits cannot be accommodated this year.
  • This year, you will only be submitting your request to register through the online registration process. Your registration is NOT confirmed until you receive a separate confirmation email in the next few weeks and have paid the registration fee.
  • Please register only members of your immediate family and only other people whose conference expenses you will be responsible for under your household. Complete a SEPARATE Registration for each family paying separately.
  • The registration fee this year is $50 per person (max $150 per family). The registration fee paid may be deducted from the total Conference fees that are due.
  • Children under 18 years of age MUST be registered by a parent or guardian who is attending the conference. A Parental/Guardian Consent Form is REQUIRED for children who are not accompanied by a parent or legal guardian. Please download the Consent Form from the conference website and email the completed form to northeastchristianconference@gmail.com
  • Cancellations: The deadline for cancellation is September 10, 2023. If you must cancel, please contact Conference Registration (northeastchristianconference@gmail.com) IMMEDIATELY so those in waiting can be accommodated.


Online registration is now closed.
